## Parties

This Contributor Agreement ("Agreement") governs voluntary contributions made to **WRDLNKDN**, a community operated by **Drake Svc LLC**, doing business as **WRDLNKDN** ("Operator").

This Agreement applies to any individual acting as a **Contributor**.

---

## Role of Contributor

A **Contributor** voluntarily assists with WRDLNKDN activities, which may include but are not limited to:

- Community moderation or facilitation  
- Content creation or curation  
- Research or synthesis  
- Event or initiative support  
- Technical or operational assistance  

The nature, scope, and duration of contributions are flexible and may change over time.

There are no required hours, deliverables, or long-term commitments.

---

## What a Contributor Is Not

Contributors are **not**:

- Owners, members, or partners  
- Employees or contractors  
- Agents or representatives  

Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, contractor, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.

---

## Ownership and Authority

WRDLNKDN is operated by **Drake Svc LLC**.

Contributors do not receive:

- Ownership or equity interest  
- Voting or governance rights  
- Authority to make decisions on behalf of WRDLNKDN or the Operator  

All final authority rests with the Operator.

---

## No Compensation

Contributions are voluntary.

Contributors do not receive:

- Wages or salaries  
- Stipends  
- Profit sharing  
- Payment for time, labor, or services  

Reimbursement of approved expenses does not constitute compensation.

---

## Expense Reimbursement

Some Contributors may incur expenses on behalf of WRDLNKDN.

Reimbursement:

- Is **not guaranteed**  
- Is **not compensation**  
- Requires documentation and approval  

Eligibility for reimbursement is governed by the **WRDLNKDN Expense Reimbursement Policy**, which is incorporated by reference.

---

## Intellectual Property

Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

- Contributors retain ownership of their pre-existing intellectual property  
- Content or materials contributed for WRDLNKDN use may be used, modified, displayed, or distributed by the Operator in furtherance of the project  

---

## Confidentiality

Contributors may have access to non-public information related to WRDLNKDN.

Contributors agree to maintain the confidentiality of non-public information obtained through participation and not to disclose it without authorization.

---

## Limitation of Authority

Contributors have no authority to:

- Bind Drake Svc LLC  
- Enter contracts  
- Incur expenses  
- Make commitments on behalf of WRDLNKDN  

Unless explicitly authorized in writing by the Operator.

---

## Termination

Contribution is voluntary and may be ended at any time by either party.

Upon termination, Contributors must stop representing themselves as affiliated with WRDLNKDN.

---

## Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which Drake Svc LLC is organized.

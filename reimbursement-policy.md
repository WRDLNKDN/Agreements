## Purpose

WRDLNKDN operates at or near cost and is supported by voluntary Contributors.

This policy defines when and how Contributors may be reimbursed for legitimate out-of-pocket expenses incurred on behalf of WRDLNKDN.

Reimbursements are intended to qualify as **accountable plan reimbursements** and are not compensation.

---

## DBA and Payment Source

WRDLNKDN operates as a **doing business as (DBA)** name of **Drake Svc LLC**.

WRDLNKDN is not a separate legal entity and does not maintain its own bank account.

All approved reimbursements are issued by **Drake Svc LLC** from its business account.

---

## Eligibility

Only Contributors with a signed **WRDLNKDN Contributor Agreement** on file may submit reimbursement requests.

Reimbursement eligibility begins only after the agreement has been received and acknowledged by the operator.

---

## Reimbursable Expenses

Expenses must be:

- Directly related to WRDLNKDN activities  
- Ordinary and necessary  
- Personally paid by the Contributor  
- Supported by receipts or documentation  

Examples of reimbursable expenses may include:

- Software or platform tools  
- Event or meeting-related costs  
- Hosting, domain, or technical services  
- Supplies purchased for community use  

---

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursable:

- Time, labor, or services  
- Stipends or flat payments  
- Profit sharing or compensation of any kind  
- Personal or unrelated expenses  
- Estimated, rounded, or undocumented costs  
- Expenses incurred without authorization when authorization was required  

Reimbursements will not exceed the actual amount paid.

---

## Submission

Reimbursement requests must be submitted by email to **wrdlnkdn@gmail.com**.

Requests must include:

- Contributor name  
- Date of expense  
- Description of WRDLNKDN-related purpose  
- Total amount  
- Receipts or proof of payment  

Requests should be submitted within **60 days** of the expense date.

## Tax Treatment

Properly documented reimbursements are intended to:

- Not be treated as income  
- Not require issuance of a W-2 or 1099  

Contributors are responsible for their own tax reporting outside of reimbursed expenses.
